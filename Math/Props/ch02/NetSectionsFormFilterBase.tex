    and let $\sigma=\{x_\alpha\}_{\alpha \in A}$ 
    be a \Net in $X$. 
    For each $\alpha \in A$, let 
    $S(\sigma, \alpha)$ denote the 
    \NetSection
    of 
    $x_\alpha$ 
    in
    $\sigma$. 
    Define 
    \begin{equation*}
    \scB=\{S(\sigma, \alpha) | \alpha \in A \}
    \end{equation*}
    Then $\scB$ is a \FilterBase on $X$.
