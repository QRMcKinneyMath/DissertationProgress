    \label{prop:ExistenceOfBalancedNeighborhoods}
    Let $(X,\T)$ be a 
    \TVS
    over a 
    \Field
    $\F$.
    The following are True. 
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item If 
            $U \in \scU_{\T}(0)$
            , then there is a 
            \BalancedSet
            $V \subset U$
            such that 
            $V \in \scU_{\T}(0)$.
        \item There exists a 
            \NeighborhoodBasis
            about $0 \in X$ 
            for $\T$ 
            consisting entirely 
            of \BalancedSet sets.

            $U \in \scU_{\T}(0)$

            %, \ConvexSet

            such that 
            $V \in \scU_{\T}(0)$.
        \item If $(X,\T)$ is 
            \LocallyConvex, 
            then there exists a 
            \NeighborhoodBasis
            about $0 \in X$ 
            for $\T$ 
            consisting entirely 
            of 
            \BalancedSet
            %, \ConvexSet 
            sets.
    \end{enumerate}
