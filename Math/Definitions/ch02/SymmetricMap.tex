    \bf \hyperref[def:Symmetricmap]{Symmetric Map} \rm
}
\begin{df}[Triangle Inequality]
    
    Let X and Y be sets. 
    We say that a map $f:X \times X \to Y$ is a \SymmetricMap for each $x_0,x_1 \in X$, $f(x_0,x_1)=f(x_1,x_0)$.
\end{df}
