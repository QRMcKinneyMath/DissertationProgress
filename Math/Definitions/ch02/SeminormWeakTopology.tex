\begin{df}[Weak Topologies Related to a TVS]
\label{def:WeakTopologyOfTVS}
\rm
Let $X$ be a \TVS.
The \weak \Topology on $X$ is understood to be the 
\WeakTopology on $X$ generated by $X^*$. 
The \weakstar \Topology on $X^*$ is understood to be the 
\WeakTopology on $X^*$ generated by $c(X)$ where $c:X \to X^{''}$ is the 
\CanonicalEmbedding.
\end{df}
