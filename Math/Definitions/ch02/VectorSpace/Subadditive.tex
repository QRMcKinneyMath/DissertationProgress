\begin{df}[\Subadditive]
\label{def:subadditive}
\rm
Let $G$ be a 
\Magma 
and 
$(H, \leq)$ 
be a 
\PartiallyOrderedMagma. 
We call a mapping $p:G \to H$ \Subadditive if, for every $x,y \in G$, 
\begin{equation*}
    p(xy) \leq p(x)p(y)
\end{equation*}
Under these circumstances, 
we may also say that
$p$
 posesses $\Subadditivity$. 
\end{df}
