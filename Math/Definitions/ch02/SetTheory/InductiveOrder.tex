\begin{df}[\InductiveOrder]
    Let $(X,\leq)$ be a \Poset.
    We say that $\leq$ is an 
    \InductiveOrder on $X$ 
    and we say that 
    $X$ is \InductivelyOrdered
    by $\leq$ if 
    each \Chain in $X$ has an \UpperBound in $X$. 
    we also call $(X,\leq)$ an 
    \InductivelyOrdered set in this circumstance.


\end{df}
