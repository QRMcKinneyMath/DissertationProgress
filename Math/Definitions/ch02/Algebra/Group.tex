\begin{df}[\Group]
\label{def:Group}
\rm
    Let $(X,\oplus,e)$ 
    be a \Monoid
    such that
    each 
    $x \in X$
    is an
    \InvertibleElement.
    Then we call
    $(X,\oplus,e)$ a 
    \Group. 
	Out of respect, we call a 
	\CommutativeGroup
	an
	\AbelianGroup.
\end{df}
