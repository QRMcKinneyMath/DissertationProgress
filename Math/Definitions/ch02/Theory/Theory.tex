\begin{df}[\Theory]
\label{def:Theory}
    
\rm
    Let \scA be an \Alphabet and let 
    \scG be a \Grammar on \scA. 
    A \Theory on \scG is a 
    subcollection of the set of \Statements of \scG. 
    We call a \Statement contained in a \Theory a 
    \Theorem of that \Theory. 
    If \scT is a \Theory and 
    $t$ is a \Theorem in \scT, then we write 
    \isthm{\scT}$t$
\end{df}
