\begin{df}[Pseudometric Complete]
    We say that a \PseudometricSpace $(X,d)$ is 
    \PseudometricComplete if each \PseudometricCauchySequence sequence in $(X,d)$ \PseudometricConverges to a limit in $X$. 
    \end{df}
