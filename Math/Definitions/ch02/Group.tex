\begin{df}[\Group]
    Let $(X,\oplus,e)$ 
    be a \Monoid
    such that
    each 
    $x \in X$
    is an
    \InvertibleElement.
    Then we call
    $(X,\oplus,e)$ a 
    \Group. 
\end{df}
