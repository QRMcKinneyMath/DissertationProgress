\begin{df}[\Magma]
    Let $X$ be a set and
    $T:X \times X \to X$ be a 
    \BinaryOperation
    on $X$. 
    We call the pair $(X,T)$ a 
    \Magma.
    When it is clear what operation is being referred to, 
    we may simply refer to $X$
    as the 
    \Magma. 
\end{df}
